A couple of months ago I gave up a fight I had been vigilantly waging for many years.

I stopped actively clearing my email inbox.

I stopped for two reasons:
1) I hit a point in my life where I've been trying to curb unproductive and net negative habits. Compulsively checking my email was one of them.
2) My email had degraded to a point where I felt that I was having to deal with a lot of spam and always unsubscribing from *some company marketing or service*.

What was once a relatively pristine site was now sitting at **8000+** unread messages. Once upon of time this would have given me nightmares, but now its just my everyday. I am the metaphorical ostrich and my head is comfortably in the sand.

But, today, email is still important. I don't want to just abandon it altogether, that would be crazy.

What I decided was that I should just build my ideal system for filtering and handling my emails. If you're like me and have a similar issue, then hopefully this article can help you assemble something similar.
